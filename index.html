// About

Rekha completed her MSW at McGill University; where she received her clinical training at the McGill Couple and Family Clinic under the mentorship of Dr. Sharon Bond.

Rekha has 15 years of experience in children’s mental health and family counseling services. In addition to her private practice, she also provides supervision to developing therapists and mentor’s graduate students through Carleton University Alumni program.

As a wife, mother, and full-time professional Rekha is grounded and pragmatic. She is knowledgeable of the local schools, children's aid, and family court system and is an effective oral and written advocate.

Rekha is equally happy to provide services as to provide an informed referral. Please review the "Services" and "F.A.Q" sections below for additional information.
